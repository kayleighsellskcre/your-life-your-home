Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I check my home value?

We recommend checking quarterly to stay informed about market changes. Your dashboard updates automatically with Homebot integration, making it effortless to track value and equity growth over time.

What's the best time to renovate for maximum ROI?

It depends on your local market and goals. Kitchen and bathroom updates typically return 60-80% of costs. Our ROI Guide helps you prioritize projects based on your specific situation and timeline.

How do I know if refinancing makes sense?

Generally, if you can reduce your rate by 0.5-0.75% and plan to stay in your home for 2+ years, refinancing may be worthwhile. Use our calculator or chat with us to run your specific numbers.

What maintenance tasks are most important?

Focus on: HVAC filter changes monthly, gutter cleaning twice yearly, and seasonal roof/foundation inspections. These prevent the most expensive repairs. See our full maintenance guide for detailed schedules.

Can I work with multiple professionals through your platform?

Yes! You can connect with agents, lenders, and other professionals who are part of your trusted team. They'll have access to share insights and updates directly through your dashboard.
