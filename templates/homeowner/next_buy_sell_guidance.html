Buy & Sell Guidance

Real estate can feel heavy. This page lays out the main steps in everyday language so you don't have to guess.

When You're Buying

  1. Clarify your timeline and comfort zone for payments
  2. Talk with a lender about pre-approval options
  3. Explore areas and home types that fit your life
  4. Tour homes with a clear list of needs and dealbreakers
  5. Write offers that protect you while staying realistic
  6. Move through inspections, appraisal, and final approval
  7. Close and settle into your new routines

When You're Selling or Doing Both

  1. Look at your equity and payoff so you know your starting point
  2. Decide what to fix, clean, or stage and what to leave alone
  3. Set a pricing strategy that matches your market reality
  4. Prepare for showings and feedback
  5. Review offers with calm eyes and clear numbers
  6. Handle inspections and repair conversations
  7. Coordinate timelines with your next home when needed

This page is a high-level overview. Your agent and lender will help you personalize the details so the plan fits your life, not someone else's.
