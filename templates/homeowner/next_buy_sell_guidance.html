When You're Buying

1

Clarify Your Timeline & Budget

Start by understanding your timeline and comfort zone for monthly payments. This foundation helps every decision that follows feel grounded, not rushed.

2

Get Pre-Approved

Talk with a trusted lender about pre-approval options. Knowing your buying power upfront gives you confidence when you find the right home.

3

Explore Areas & Home Types

Discover neighborhoods and property types that align with your lifestyle. Consider schools, commute, amenities, and the feeling you want your home to give you.

4

Tour Homes Strategically

Visit properties with a clear list of needs and dealbreakers. Take notes, ask questions, and trust your instincts about what feels right.

5

Write Strong Offers

Craft offers that protect your interests while staying realistic about market conditions. Your agent will help balance competitiveness with your comfort level.

6

Navigate Inspections & Appraisal

Move through inspections, appraisal, and final loan approval with your team. Each step brings you closer to closing with confidence.

7

Close & Settle In

Complete the closing process and begin settling into your new home. This is where your vision becomes reality.

When You're Selling or Doing Both

1

Understand Your Equity Position

Review your equity and loan payoff so you know your starting point. This clarity helps you make informed decisions about pricing and timing.

2

Prepare Your Home

Decide what to fix, clean, or stage‚Äîand what to leave alone. Strategic preparation maximizes appeal without over-investing.

3

Set Your Pricing Strategy

Establish a pricing approach that matches your market reality. Your agent will help you balance speed, profit, and your personal goals.

4

Host Showings & Gather Feedback

Prepare for showings and welcome constructive feedback. Each showing is an opportunity to refine your approach.

5

Review Offers Thoughtfully

Evaluate offers with calm eyes and clear numbers. Consider not just price, but terms, timeline, and buyer strength.

6

Handle Inspections & Repairs

Navigate inspection results and repair conversations with transparency. Your agent will help you negotiate fair solutions.

7

Coordinate Your Next Move

When selling and buying simultaneously, coordinate timelines carefully. Your team will help create a seamless transition.

Remember: This guide provides a high-level overview. Your agent and lender will help you personalize every detail so the plan fits your life, your timeline, and your goals‚Äînot someone else's template.
